What is Adventurer's Journal?

Ever been so attached to a character that you couldn't get enough time at the table to roleplay them without completely alienating every other player at the table? This is a place for roleplaying away from the game table. Not a cure for character addiction, but an avenue for slight remission of symptoms. Sign up (or log in), and talk about whatever as your character.
